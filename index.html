╔═════════ ≪ °❈ About This Site ❈° ≫ ═════════╗

This website was built as my submission to the Codedex "Mini Holiday Hackathon" which took part on December 13-15, 2024.

For this hackathon, I chose Track 2: Now, it’s Personal

I have always had a great love for the internet, specifically, having my own custom corner of the web. This corner has changed, not just as I have grown up, but with platform and social changes. Forums, MSN, Tumblr, Xanga, Blogger, Twitter; blogging and micro-blogging have always had my heart.

I hope I have been able to make you feel nostalgic whether you have experienced dial up or not.
