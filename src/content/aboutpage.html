This website was built as my submission to the Codedex "Mini Holiday Hackathon" which took part on December 13-15, 2024.
You can find the repo here.


The Purpose of this site (per Codedex Challenge): Capture the fun, homebrew vibe of the early internet, when everyone had their own little corner of the web. This is YOUR design, let your retro flag fly!
Grading Criteria: How well does the project capture personal creativity or nostalgia? Does the project offer engaging or entertaining features for visitors?

The Design: Was on a time crunch for this, but I really wanted to capture the experience of surfing the web 'back in the day', but still use some modern comforts. If you choose to view this on a mobile device, you are getting a 21st century experience, but hopefully the *vibes* are still here. Viewing from a desktop? Welcome to the world before sites needed to be responsive. Welcome to whitespace! Welcome to viewing a page that was built for a square screen!
